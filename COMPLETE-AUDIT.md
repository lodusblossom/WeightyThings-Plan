## Key Findings

### 1. Internal Linking is Poor
- Most posts have NO internal links
- Only Pool Floaties and Patio Chairs link to each other
- This hurts SEO — site needs to be interconnected

### 2. Affiliate Links Need Verification
- Many links are from 2020, may be broken or redirected
- Some posts link to direct retailer sites (Torrid, Walmart) instead of Amazon
- Need to verify all Amazon links still work and are properly tagged

### 3. Content is Well-Written
- Voice is genuine and helpful
- Personal stories add value
- Products have good descriptions with specs

### 4. Some Posts Have No Affiliate Potential
- "Fat Visual Artists" — no products
- "Plus Size Influencers" — no products
- These are engagement posts, not money posts

### 5. Mix of Post Types
- Product reviews (good for affiliate)
- List posts (engagement, no affiliate)
- Personal essays (brand building)
