## Content Quality Checklist (for each post)

Before publishing, each post must have:

```
□ H1 Title (includes keyword)
□ Meta title (50-60 chars)
□ Meta description (150-160 chars)
□ Introduction (what this post covers, why it matters)
□ Quick Decision Guide (at top)
□ 5-8 product recommendations with:
    - Product name
    - Price
    - Link (Amazon Associates tagged)
    - Pros
    - Cons
    - Who it's for
□ Each product link uses YOUR affiliate tag
□ FAQ section (minimum 5 questions)
□ Internal links to 2-3 related posts
□ Conclusion
□ Disclosure (I earn from qualifying purchases)
□ Schema markup (Product or Review)
```
